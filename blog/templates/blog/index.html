Society of Women Engineers NYIT Manhattan Chapter

Our Mission:

Stimulate women to achieve full potential in careers as engineers and leaders, expand the image of the engineering profession as a positive force in improving the quality of life, and demonstrate the value of diversity.

About us:

For more than six decades, SWE has given women engineers a unique place and voice within the engineering industry. Our organization is centered around a passion for our members' success and continues to evolve with the challenges and opportunities reflected in today's exciting engineering and technology specialties. We invite you to explore the values, principles, and priorities that guide our initiatives and learn how together, SWE can continue to make a lasting impact on the future.

Our Events

Our SWE chapter is popular for hosting events that encourage everyone to participate. Not only technical workshops, but a large variety of social events that will suit everyone are presented. The events are categorized in: technical workshops, social events, conferences, collaborations with other clubs

Technical Workshops

ACM and IEEE host various of workshops for students to learn more in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. This semester: PCB, RPi, Arduino, VR, Python, Web-dev and others.

Social Events

Engineering clubs not only host tech related events, but also have various of social events where members of UE just spend quality time together.

Conference

All clubs organize trips to their regional and national conferences. Most popular conference is NSBE regional, where members of NSBE get an opportunity to apply for jobs.

Collabs

All of the United Engineering Clubs (ACM, IEEE, NSBE and SWE) collaborate on the events to ensure the best outcome for the student body.
